Purchase TI-84 Calculator with GB Game Installed

This TI-84 calculator has been custom-modified to play GameBoy games, so you can enjoy both math and gaming on the go! The gameplay is functional, though it may not be as clear or smooth as on more powerful devices, and there's no option to save your progress. For a better gaming experience, we recommend the TI-89. If you’d like a specific GameBoy game(s) installed, feel free to email us with your request. Please note that not all games may be compatible, but we’ll give it a try—if it doesn’t work, we can always try another option.
